About Me

I am Weldamlak Ayenew Endalew. I have been a servant at Abune Gebre Menfes Kidus Church, where for the past two to three years I have been learning and teaching the doctrines of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo faith. Alongside this service, I practice art drawing as both a hobby and a form of spiritual reflection. The artworks presented here are inspired by the teachings I have received. Each drawing carries a deeper religious meaning and serves as a visual expression of what I have learned through faith, study, and service.
